-- Agatha's Scavenger Hunt -->

Agatha, welcome to the great ocean
Where the animals are causing quite a commotion
To make your way back to land
A set of riddles will lend you a hand
The clues will lead you to different locations
where you'll find underwater animals like crustaceans
then you'll type their names into this website
until you reach the end of this plight!
